Flagships

Aminah Therapeutic Garden (ATG)

ATG is an effort by Mahallah Aminah to support SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being and SDG 15: Life on Land.

Enjoy mini garden, a mini bird park, a reflexology path, edible herbal plants, a sitting area and a small pond.

The produce from our edible garden are free for the residents to pick and also are up for sale.

This garden is hoped to bring some joy and peace of mind to residents while enjoying the view.

Sustainable Kelulut Ecosystem

Aminah Madu Kelulut project is part of Mahallah Aminah's initiative to support SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities.

In collaboration with Kesatuan Kakitangan Sokongan Satu, IIUM (KESATU), we intended to promote the consumption of natural honey produced by the heterotrigona itama bee which contain numerous health benefits- renew aging cells, rich in natural antibiotics as well as provide nutrients such as antioxidants.

Goal: To produce honey-based products for sale among the IIUM community to empower our B40 residents' entrepreneurship efforts.

Mahallah Aminah Healthy Lifestyle

This project is inline with SDG 3: Good Health and Well Being and SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities.

MAHaL is a project initiative in collaboration with the Sports Development Center (SDC) to promote healthy lifestyle and healthy living through recreational events among mahallah residents.

Objectives: Provide an outdoor recreational park for the residents, fill up the students' free time and build a healthy environment within mahallah.

Initiatives: A calorie calculator walkway, a woodball arena and traditional games arena.
